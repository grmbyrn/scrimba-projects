Visit Dublin

A small capital with a huge reputation, Dublin has a mix of heritage and hedonism that will not disappoint.

Top three activities to do in Dublin

Attend a show or concert in the Abbey

Along with boasting an illustrious history, it still regularly produces plays from contemporary and historical writers.

Go for a picnic in Pheonix Park

A popular haunt of locals with dogs, Phoenix Park is home to both a herd of deer and the Irish president, who lives at Áras an Uachtaráin.

Pay a visit to the Guinness Storehouse

Learn about the Guinness history, then have a pint at the Gravity Bar, which offers excellent views over the city.
